Research

To start this journey, I grew up near the first oil well on Earth (Titusville, PA), passionate about energy & biotech from a young age. I have spent my life working across biotech (longevity, foundation models, polygenic risk scores, clinical trials, neural implants), machine learning (remote sensing, Markov chain Monte Carlo, recurrent neural networks, vision transformers), and energy systems (climate loss forecasting, extreme weather, weather derivative pricing, policy sentiment).
